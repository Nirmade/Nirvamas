About Me

Born bilingual, I have had a love affair with words and languages since childhood. It grew deep and made me multilingual; I have worked in many fields before settling on painting and writing, with a degree in journalism; I spent a few years working as such after receiving a Social Sciences degree from New York University, I worked for New York States for more than seven years, then a few years ago left this job to explore my entrepreneurial spirit I created a jewelry business with my sister Evenly Blue Jewelry and Publishing House and a translation service on my own; just saying I enjoy putting my skills to help others for a fee, in short I am a professional translator. And now I am relentlessly pursuing a career as a writer and painter and see where this new road could lead me. I hope you will join me and follow my journey.

As a writer, I have already written many books: poetry, novella, and Children's Books. All are listed on my Book page with a summary, and I also inserted a link to my Amazon page where you can buy anything you fancy. As a painter, I have yet to do a showing, but with more than five years of experience, I work on canvas as I do my work digitally and can sell the original piece or as A non-fungible token (NFT)- at your convenience.
